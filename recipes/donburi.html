Teriyaki Donburi

Description

This recipe makes a delicious teriyaki donburi (rice bowl) dish!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Chicken
  • Teriyaki Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the rice in a rice cooker
  2. Add oil to pan
  3. fry chicken till partially cooked through
  4. Add teriyaki sauce
  5. serve in bowl on rice!
